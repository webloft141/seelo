Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 24, 2026

1. Information We Collect

Account Data: When you sign in with Firebase Authentication, we receive your email address and Firebase UID. We do not store passwords.

Design Data: Design frames, images, and node information are transmitted through our relay server solely for live preview. This data is held in server memory only while the session is active and is discarded on disconnect.

Usage Analytics: We track aggregate session counts and preview counts to measure Service usage. This data is anonymized where possible.

Device Information: The mobile app collects device model and screen resolution to render previews accurately. This information is not stored permanently.

2. How We Use Information

  • To provide and maintain the Service (live preview, device management).
  • To enforce device limits and license key restrictions.
  • To improve the Service through aggregated analytics.
  • To communicate important updates or security notices.

3. Data Storage & Retention

Design data is ephemeral — stored in memory during an active session and deleted when the session ends. Account information is stored via Firebase Authentication. License keys and plan status are stored in a local JSON file on the relay server. We do not use a traditional database.

4. Third-Party Services

  • Firebase (Google): Authentication and Crashlytics. See Firebase Privacy Policy.
  • Render: Relay server hosting. See Render Privacy Policy.
  • GitHub: Open-source components. See GitHub Privacy Statement.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell or share your personal data with third parties for marketing purposes. Design data is never shared outside the active session participants.

6. Security

Communications between the plugin, relay server, and mobile app are encrypted via HTTPS and WSS. Firebase ID tokens are used for authentication. However, no service is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

7. Your Rights

You may request deletion of your account data by contacting support. You may also sign out and unlink your Firebase account at any time. Analytics data cannot be individually deleted once aggregated.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Material changes will be communicated via the Service. Continued use after changes constitutes acceptance.

9. Contact

For privacy-related inquiries, reach out via the Seelo Figma plugin or the official support channels.
